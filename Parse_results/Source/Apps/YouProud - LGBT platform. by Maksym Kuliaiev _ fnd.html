Description

YouProud – totally free progressive LGBT platform. YouProud is an international social network, which helps to find like-thinkers all over the world. Live free, feel free! Be yourself without borders and restrictions! Join widest LGBT community: - Modern society without prejudice - No limits with build in translator for simple communication - Convenient locator to find friends beside you - Make your photos perfect with numerous filters - News feed – see all your friend’s updates Your safety is very important for us. Therefore, we monitor the security and preservation of your data. We have added «Block» of a user if you decided to stop communication. We stay intouch 24/7 to help you with any issues. And more: - Modern messenger - Asses to the list of LGBT–friendly places worldwide and see all the most popular locations all over the world. - Organize your events today - have you ever wanted to spend wonderful moments in the company of like-thinkers? With YouProud you can do it anytime. Create the meeting, find new friends and enjoy moments together. Feel free to be yourself worldwide with YouProud!
